About Slym Gyms

Slym Gyms

Slym Gyms is a premier fitness center located in the heart of New York City. Founded in 2000, we have been providing our members with the ultimate workout experience for over two decades. Our state-of-the-art facility features top-of-the-line equipment, expert trainers, and a variety of group fitness classes to suit all fitness levels and goals.

At Slym Gyms, we believe that fitness is not just about looking good, but feeling good too. That's why we focus on creating a welcoming and supportive environment where our members can feel comfortable and motivated to achieve their fitness goals. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, our team is dedicated to helping you reach your full potential and become the best version of yourself. Join us at Slym Gyms and discover what it means to truly feel strong, confident, and empowered.

Our Mission Statement

At Slym Gyms, our mission is to provide the highest quality Fitness services to our customers while maintaining a commitment to sustainability, innovation, and social responsibility. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients and partners through transparency, integrity, and exceptional customer service.

Our History

Slym Gyms was founded in 2000 by Jim Slym, with a vision to create a premier fitness center that caters to the diverse needs of New York City residents. Since then, we have grown into the best gym of New York City, serving customers across the city. We are proud of our 20+ years of experience and our commitment to excellence in everything we do.

Our Team

At Slym Gyms, our team is made up of passionate, dedicated fitness professionals who are committed to delivering the best possible Fitness services to our customers. With years of experience in the industry and a deep understanding of our clients' needs, our team works tirelessly to ensure that every member achieves their fitness goals in a supportive and motivating environment.

Our Awards and Recognition

Slym Gyms is proud to have been recognized as the best gym of New York City for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and social responsibility. These achievements are a testament to our team's hard work and dedication, and we are honored to be recognized as a leader in the fitness industry.

FAQ

What kind of fitness classes does Slym Gyms offer?
We offer a wide range of fitness classes including yoga, pilates, cardio, strength training, and more.
What are your gym hours?
Our gym is open 7 days a week, from 5:00am to 10:00pm on weekdays, and from 8:00am to 8:00pm on weekends.
Is personal training available at Slym Gyms?
Yes, we offer personalized one-on-one training with certified personal trainers to help you achieve your fitness goals.
What is your cancellation policy for memberships?
We have a flexible cancellation policy that allows members to cancel their memberships at any time, without any fees or penalties.
Is there a free trial period available for new members?
Yes, we offer a free trial period for new members to try out our gym and experience our facilities and services.
Do you offer any discounts for students or seniors?
Yes, we offer special discounted rates for students and seniors. Please inquire at our front desk for more information.
What kind of equipment does Slym Gyms have?
We have a variety of state-of-the-art equipment including treadmills, ellipticals, weight machines, free weights, and more to cater to all fitness levels and preferences.
